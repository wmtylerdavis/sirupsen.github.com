The Coffee is Always Better at Home

25 June 2013

For the past year I’ve had a Starbucks within walking distance of work, that being said I’m not talking about that type work environment. We’ve all had the job with the $5-a-month coffee pot. The one where you have no clue where the coffee comes from, who makes it, or why it smells like burnt bacon. The one where you leave a skim layer of coffee at the bottom so you aren’t responsible for making the next pot, only for the guy behind you to curse the heavens and feel that yes you still should be responsible for the next pot. Maybe your office has a Keurig but you still haven’t found that perfect K-cup (it’s called JetFuel and it’s made by CoffeePeople BTW). We all know the coffee is always better at home, even if it isn’t.
