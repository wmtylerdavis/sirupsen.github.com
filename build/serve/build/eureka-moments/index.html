Eureka Moments

24 June 2013

So another reason I believe hours are bullsh**… Problems are never solved by sitting at a desk for 9 hours a day - at least not the problems I’ve come across. I tend to bang my head against my computer for hours on end trying to fix a bug only to find that the answer comes naturally to me while I’m in the throes of something else. That being said it’s not usually answering email or writing a Word doc or playing with a spreadsheet. Eureka moments happen when you’re taking a shower, walking your dog, or working out. Step away from the desk, far away, seriously - like out the door and through the woods. The solutions will find you and they aren’t necessarily on Stack Overflow.
